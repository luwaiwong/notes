# Definition
Let $X$ and $Y$ be two *random variables*. The [Correlation Coefficient](.md) $\rho(X,Y)$ is defined to be $0$ if $Var(X)=0$ or $Var(Y)=0$, and otherwise: $$\rho(X,Y)=\frac{Cov(X,Y)}{\sqrt{Var(X)Var(Y)}}$$
