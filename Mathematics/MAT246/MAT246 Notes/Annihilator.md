For any subset $U\subseteq V$, the **Annihilator** of $U$, denoted $U^{0}$ is:
$$U^{0}=\{\varphi\in V':\varphi(u)=0 \text{ for all } u\in U\}$$
Properties
- $U^{0}$ is a subspace of $V'$
- $\dim U^{0}=\dim V-\dim U$
- $U^{0}=\{0\}\iff U=V$
- $U^{0}=V'\iff U=\{0\}$
