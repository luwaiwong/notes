The **Fix Set** of an element $g$ (Given $G\curvearrowright X$ and let $g\in G$) is:
$$X^{g}=\{x\in X: g\ .x=x\}$$

> Note:
> 	Fix-sets are *not* the same as [Stabilizers](Stabilizer.md); $X^{g}\subseteq X$ while $G_{x}\leq G$
